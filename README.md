Understand how the company gets loans.

Suppliers give us minerals, but sometimes we have not paid yet. This is money we owe to suppliers.

Sometimes we give money to suppliers in advance, and later they provide minerals for the money we already paid.

Buyers are people who purchase our minerals. Sometimes buyers give us advance money before taking minerals, or sometimes they take minerals and pay later.

Add expenses and keep track of all transactions in `journal.php`.

Please check whether these scenarios have been properly handled.
